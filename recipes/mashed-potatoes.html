Mashed Potatoes

Smooth and creamy mashed potatoes are the perfect comfort dish! An amazing and filling side for any meal, this recipe is sure to come out perfect every time!

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds potatoes
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/3 cup melted salted butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • salt to taste
  • black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Peel and quarter potatoes, place in a pot of cold salted water.
  2. Add cloves of garlic and bring to a boil, cook uncovered 15 minutes or until fork tender. Drain well.
  3. Heat milk on the stove top (or in the microwave) until warm.
  4. Add butter to the potatoes and and begin mashing. Pour in heated milk a little at a time while using a potato masher to reach desired consistency.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot.
